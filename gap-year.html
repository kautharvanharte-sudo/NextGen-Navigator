Gap Year Resources

Explore opportunities to travel, volunteer, and grow during your gap year:

    The world’s largest provider of international volunteering and internships, Projects Abroad offers life-changing experiences in over 20 countries. Whether you're passionate about education, conservation, medicine, or community development, their programs are designed to make a real impact while supporting the UN Sustainable Development Goals. With 24/7 support and a hassle-free setup, you can focus on learning, growing, and giving back.

  • Projects Abroad

    • Connect with hosts around the world offering free accommodation in exchange for help with projects. Great for cultural immersion and budget-friendly travel.

  • Workaway

    • The official nonprofit supporting gap year education and advocacy. Find accredited programs, planning guides, scholarships, and expert advice to help you design a transformative and intentional gap year experience.
